Questions, Answered

Is this legitimate? How can I be sure the money actually goes to charity? +

Yes, Charity Stream is legitimate—and we're glad the idea feels almost too good to be true. All net revenue goes to verified 501(c)(3) charities. We publish monthly reports with receipts, run a live donation tracker, and share partner confirmations. Don't take our word for it—check the Impact tab to hear directly from the charities we've worked with.

How does charity stream work, and why would advertisers pay for this? +

Charity Stream turns ad views into donations. Advertisers pay to run campaigns, viewers actively choose to engage, and all net revenue goes to verified 501(c)(3) charities selected weekly by the community. For businesses, it's cheaper than traditional ads, builds a socially responsible brand image, and can be a tax-deductible expense.

How do you stay in business if you donate everything, and how do you ensure ad views are genuine? +

Simply put, Charity Stream was built for impact, not profit. We only cover the essentials—hosting, processing, and compliance—while 100% of net revenue goes directly to verified charities. To protect our community, we use safeguards like 2FA, fraud detection, and activity monitoring, ensuring every donation reflects genuine engagement. Because at Charity Stream, we believe value lives in what you share, not what you keep.

What does this cost me, and how do I start? +

It's completely free to make a difference—that's a promise we stand by. Just click *Start Watching* and begin making an impact right away. For those who want more, an optional $1/month premium unlocks fewer pop-ups, external browser viewing, speed controls, and 1080p quality—and even that dollar goes directly to charity. Simple as that: no strings, no catch—your time creates benefit, at zero cost to you.

Which charities get the donations, and can I choose? +

Donations go to verified 501(c)(3) organizations we've vetted for transparency, mission alignment, and impact. Each week, a featured charity is chosen through community voting, and all net donations for that week go directly to them. You'll always see which charity your views supported, and you can help shape future selections by joining the vote.

Why not just donate money directly? +

Direct donations are amazing, and you absolutely should if you have the means. But Charity Stream transforms something everyone controls—their screen time—and monetizes it collectively for good without changing your budget or routine. With leaderboards, real-time tracking, and community features, giving becomes engaging and collective, bringing in far more people than traditional donations alone would. We're building an ecosystem where brands do good publicly, charities get needed exposure, and your everyday habits help create extraordinary impact.
