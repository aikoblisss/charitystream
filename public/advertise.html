How it Works

For Companies

Flexible Donation-Based Advertising

Advertisement slots are paid for on a CPM (cost per 1,000 views) donation basis—you choose what your message is worth. Set your own rate from $0.01 to $100 per 1,000 views.

Simple Process:
  1. Submit your ad with your chosen CPM rate
  2. We review for quality and community guidelines
  3. Get confirmation when your ad goes live
  4. Track impact as your budget becomes charitable donations

One curated ad reel plays each week featuring video and static ads from partner organizations. All revenue goes directly to the week's featured charity—no exceptions.

For Charities

Weekly Charity Selection

Each week, one charity receives 100% of our advertising revenue for maximum impact.

Entry Process:
  1. Submit an entry – Provide charity name, email, EIN, and a $1 entry fee (donated to the chosen charity).
  2. Random selection – Three charities are drawn from all verified entries.
  3. Community vote – Users vote for their preferred charity among the three.
  4. Charity selected – The chosen charity receives the full week's ad revenue plus all entry fees.

Must be a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization and comply with our charity partner community guidelines.
